What is Deep Learning?

Deep Learning is a more complex form of Machine Learning, which uses complex algorithms such as Convoluted Neural Network (CNN), to provide accurate and adaptive predictions. 

Advantages of Deep Learning

Deep Learning offers numerous advantages, including automated feature learning, high accuracy, scalability, transfer learning, parallel processing, handling non-linearity, etc. It continues to evolve with ongoing research, making it a versatile & powerful tool for tackling complex tasks in diverse domains,
